# The codebook of the tidydata.txt

### SubjectID

Type: integer

Description: ID of the subject whose data are collected(1-30)

### ActivityLabel

Type: character

Description: Name of the activity labels that the subject is doing. Should be one of 

1. WALKING
1. WALKING_UPSTAIRS
1. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
1. SITTING
1. STANDING
1. LAYING
